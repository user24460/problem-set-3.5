Instagram: The King of Social Media Platforms

By: Celson Gutkowski

Click here to access Instagram's homepage.

Features

Below are some of Instagram's features that will be further elaborated upon.

  1. Pages
  2. Stories
  3. Feed

Pages

The personal page is one of Instagram's main features. On the personal page, the user's username, biography, and contact information is displayed. In addition, the personal page displays the number of people that the user follows or is followed by. All users are given the ability to post photos or videos with a caption, tag, and/or location. The photo or video will then be displayed onto the user's personal page, where followers can then like and add comments to the post.

Stories

Similar to Snapchat, the user is given the ability to post photos or videos onto a personalized story that can then be viewed by other users. While there are options to customize these photos or videos before adding them to stories, Snapchat's customizing options are, in most cases, superior. Nonetheless, both platforms' story feature holds the same purpose: to quickly share photos or videos with other users.

Feed

In order to keep up to date with others, Instagram displays posts by other users on a constantly updating feed. The user can then scroll through this feed to like and comment on posts. Instead of displaying other users' posts in chronological order, Instagram uses an algorithm that orders them based on relevance to the user.

My Experience

Instagram has had a mixed effect on my life. It has brought positive change in that I am able to express myself through my page and see what my friends are up to by viewing their stories. Additionally, I have made new friends that I keep in contact with through Instagram. It has brought negative change in that I compare myself to others based on their photos, even though I realize that what they post are merely highlights of their real life. Furthermore, Instagram is extremely addictive and I sometimes spend hours exploring other users' pages when I could be doing something more productive. However, I still believe that Instagram is a wonderful platform to express yourself through, which is why I rate it a 4/5.
