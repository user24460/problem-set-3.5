My Experience with Social Media

By: Celson Gutkowski

As a result of the technological boom that humanity is currently undergoing, the way that we connect to each other has been revolutionized. With the advent of social media, we have the ability to meet new people with the snap of a finger. The following information found in this piece discusses some of the features of a select few social media platforms. Additionally, I will be discussing my experience with these platforms as a teenager living in the 21st Century.

Instagram

Instagram is a social media platform that allows users to post their images or videos on a customizable personal page. Each user has the ability to follow other users in order to keep up to date with their posts. Additionally, users can discover other users' pages through an explore feature, a personalized hub that suggests pages that may interest the user based on their activity.

Snapchat

Snapchat is a social media platform that allows users to take and edit short videos to post on a personalized story. Other users are then able to view and respond to this story. Additionally, the platform's chat feature allows two or more users to contact each other through messages, images, or videos. Each user has the ability to "add" other users in order to keep up to date with their stories.

VSCO

VSCO is a social media platform that has similar features to Instagram. It gives users the ability to share images or videos in a customizable personal page. However, VSCO allows users to upload and edit their images without having to post them. VSCO's photo-editing feature also gives users more customizing options to match their aesthetic. Each user has the ability to follow another user to keep up to date with their posts.
