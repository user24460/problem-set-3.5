My Experience with Social Media

By: Celson Gutkowski

As a result of the technological boom that humanity is currently undergoing, the way that we connect to each other has been revolutionized. With the advent of social media, we have the ability to meet new people with the snap of a finger.

The following information found in this piece will discuss some of the features of a select few social media platforms. Additionally, I will be discussing my experience with these platforms as a teenager living in the 21st Century.

Instagram

Instagram is a social media platform that allows users to post their images or videos on a customizable personal page. Each user has the ability to follow other users in order to keep up to date with their posts. Additionally, users can discover other users' pages through an explore feature and contact each other through direct messages

Snapchat

Snapchat is a social media platform that allows users to take short videos and edit these videos to post on a personalized story. Additionally, Snapchat's chat feature allows two or more users to contact each other through messages and images or videos. Each users has the ability to add other users in order to keep up to date with their stories.

VSCO

VSCO is a social media platform that has similar features to Instagram. It gives users the ability to share images or videos in a customizable personal page. However, VSCO allows users to upload and edit their images without having to post them. Each user has the ability to follow each other users to keep up to date with their posts.
